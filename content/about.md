**Welcome to my space**

Photography, for me, is a way to hold onto moments that feel too fleeting to lose.  
With the arrival of my children, I found myself capturing more — preserving not just images, but *atmosphere*, *mood*, and the *beauty* I notice in everyday life.

Today, I am learning to **slow down**, to **observe more carefully**, and to create **fewer images** — more thoughtful and intentional, meant to last beyond the moment as part of a growing family archive.  
This practice became even more meaningful after I started working with a **Fujifilm camera**, which encourages a more deliberate and attentive way of seeing.

What you see here is not a professional portfolio, but a **personal way of seeing**.

Thank you for visiting my website — I hope you enjoy exploring my visual world.
